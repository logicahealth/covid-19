StructureDefinition: COVID18Diagnosis

A diagnosis or diagnoses asserted about the subject that is due to infection by the SARS coronavirus 2 virus.

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://fhir.logicahealth.org/covid19/StructureDefinition/COVID-19-Diagnosis
